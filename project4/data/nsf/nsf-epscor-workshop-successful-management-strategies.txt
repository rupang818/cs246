              A workshop on management strategies and future planning for the NSF EPSCoR
              program will be held in June
2003.  Topics to be addressed include the
              following:  strategies for building effective state committees, the role of
              EPSCoR in economic development, human resource development at research
              universities, effective evaluation strategies, effective communication with the
              public, and prioritizing areas for EPSCoR investment.  The resulting
              documnetation will be a resource manual and a set of recommendations for future
              strategies.
